Elite Rackz is dedicated to delivering engineered solutions that enhance warehouse safety, efficiency, and long-term reliability. We specialize in pallet rack repair, guarding systems, and a full range of warehouse protection products designed to safeguard both operations and personnel. Every solution is developed with precision engineering and is backed by a comprehensive lifetime warranty, ensuring enduring value and confidence for our customers.

As part of our continued growth and commitment to excellence, Elite Rackz has acquired FBS Group, Inc. This strategic acquisition allows us to build on an established legacy of quality while broadening our capabilities to meet the evolving needs of our customers. By combining proven expertise with forward-thinking innovation, Elite Rackz has positioned itself as a trusted partner in warehouse safety across industries.

David Enriquez, CEO

David Enriquez brings over 20 years of hands‑on experience in the warehouse racking and repair industry. Over two decades, he has designed, installed, and managed countless repair and safety solutions for facilities across North America. Mentored by some of the best in the industry, David’s approach combines engineered precision with real‑world practicality, always centered on safety and long‑term performance.

To expand capabilities and serve customers end‑to‑end, David recently acquired FBS Group, Inc.—a respected provider of warehouse equipment solutions. This strategic step strengthens Elite Rackz’s supply, installation, and service offering while reinforcing the company’s commitment to quality and speed.

Beyond the job site, David is a devoted family man with a wonderful wife and children. He leads with humility, accountability, and a constant drive to improve—always striving for greatness.
